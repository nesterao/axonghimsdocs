## Description
**Character Limit: 4000 characters**

GHIMS Monitor is the essential mobile application for field trainers in Ghana's Health Information Management System. Designed to streamline deployment management, shift tracking, and incident reporting, GHIMS Monitor empowers health system field staff to work efficiently in any environment.

**Key Features:**

• **Offline-First Capability** - Work seamlessly without internet connection. All data automatically syncs when connectivity is restored, ensuring no information is lost.

• **GPS-Based Clock-In/Out** - Accurate shift tracking with location verification. Clock in and out from facility locations with automatic proximity validation for complete accountability.

• **Deployment Management** - View team assignments, facility locations, and deployment details at a glance. Stay informed about your current deployment and team members.

• **Incident Reporting** - Quickly document field incidents with detailed descriptions and photo attachments. Submit incidents with confidence knowing they're securely stored.

• **Shift & Progress Tracking** - Monitor work schedules, track active shifts, and view progress on assigned tasks. Real-time updates keep teams coordinated.

• **Comprehensive Reporting** - Generate and manage daily reports with attachments. Access historical reports and track completion status.

• **Real-Time Notifications** - Receive instant alerts for important updates, deployment changes, and system notifications.

• **Secure Authentication** - Phone-based login with secure password management ensures only authorized personnel access the system.

• **Background Auto-Clock-Out** - Never forget to clock out. The app automatically clocks out users after shift end times.

**Benefits:**

- Improve accountability and transparency in field operations
- Reduce data loss with automatic offline syncing
- Increase efficiency with streamlined workflows
- Enhance decision-making with real-time data access
- Support health system training and deployment initiatives

GHIMS Monitor is built for Ghana's unique healthcare needs, with support for the Ghana phone number format and optimized for mobile networks in the region.

**Requirements:**

- iOS 12.0 or later
- Location services enabled for GPS-based clock-in/out
- Minimum 50MB free storage
- Authorised GHIMS account (provided by Axon Information Systems)
