Getting Started

Initial Setup

  • Download GHIMS Monitor from the App Store
  • Launch the app and log in with your 10-digit Ghana phone number and password
  • Grant location services permission when prompted — required for clock-in/out
  • Your deployment and facility details load automatically after login

Enable Location Services

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  • Ensure Location Services is turned on
  • Find GHIMS Monitor and set it to "While Using the App" or "Always"
  • Return to the app — GPS clock-in will now be available

Troubleshooting

Issue: Can't log in

  • Ensure you're using the correct 10-digit phone number starting with 0
  • Check that Caps Lock is off when entering your password
  • Verify your account is active — contact your administrator if unsure
  • If locked out, your administrator can reset your credentials

Issue: GPS clock-in not working

  • Enable Location Services in iOS Settings (see Getting Started above)
  • Wait up to 30 seconds for GPS to acquire a signal
  • Verify you are within 100 metres of the facility entrance
  • Move to an open area if signal is blocked by a building

Issue: Tasks not loading

  • Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular data)
  • Pull down to refresh the Tasks list
  • Restart the app if the list remains empty
  • Contact your Team Lead to verify tasks have been assigned to you

Issue: Data not syncing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app — pending operations sync automatically on launch
  • Toggle airplane mode off and on to refresh connectivity
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space (50MB minimum)
