Empower Your Field Teams

Axon GHIMS Monitor is the dedicated mobile app for field trainers in Ghana's Health Information Management System. Log shifts with GPS verification, manage assigned tasks, and stay in sync with your team — even without an internet connection.

Core Features

🕒 Shift Management & GPS Clock-In/Out

Accurately record working hours at your assigned facility with location-verified clock-ins and automatic shift approvals.

  • Clock in and out using GPS — verified within 100 metres of your facility
  • Shift records captured with precise timestamps and GPS coordinates
  • Automatic clock-out if you leave the facility without logging out
  • Shifts sent to your Team Lead for approval after clock-out
  • Receive notifications on approval or rejection with reviewer notes
  • View full shift history including total work time and break minutes

✅ Task Management & Delegation

Stay on top of your responsibilities and, if you are a Team Lead, manage and delegate work to your team.

  • View all tasks assigned to you in one place
  • Filter by status: Assigned, Delegated, Completed, Submitted, Cancelled
  • Set task priorities: Low, Medium, High, or Critical
  • Submit task completion reports directly from the app
  • Team Leads: Create, delegate, and track tasks across the team
  • Track deadlines and see who completed what

📡 Offline-First Architecture

GHIMS Monitor is built to work in low-connectivity environments common in Ghana's field deployments.

  • All operations (clock-in, clock-out, task updates) saved locally when offline
  • Automatic background sync when internet connectivity is restored
  • No data is ever lost due to poor or absent network coverage
  • Pending operations queue managed transparently in the background
  • Connectivity status visible at a glance within the app
